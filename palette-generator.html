How to Use This Tool

1

Pick Your Base Color

Use the color picker, enter hex values, adjust sliders, or click on the color wheel to select your base color. The tool shows RGB, HSL, CMYK, and HSV values in real-time.

2

Generate Color Harmony

Select a harmony rule (Complementary, Analogous, Triadic, etc.) to automatically generate a cohesive color palette based on color theory principles.

3

Check Accessibility

Review WCAG 2.1 compliance for text contrast ratios. The tool automatically checks all color combinations in your palette and provides ratings (AAA, AA, Fail).

4

Customize Your Palette

Add or remove colors, adjust individual colors, rename your palette, and save your favorite combinations for future reference.

5

Export and Use

Export your palette in various formats including CSS variables, SCSS variables, JSON, and Tailwind CSS configuration for easy integration into your projects.
